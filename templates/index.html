Feature Guide (Mean Morphology Measures)

Enter numeric values based on your measurement method. These are mean morphology descriptors.

  • radius_mean: average radius (size).
  • texture_mean: intensity variation (texture).
  • perimeter_mean: boundary length.
  • area_mean: area size.
  • smoothness_mean: boundary smoothness.
  • compactness_mean: shape compactness.
  • concavity_mean: inward curve severity.
  • concave points_mean: count of concave points.
  • symmetry_mean: symmetry score.
  • fractal_dimension_mean: boundary complexity.
